I am a PhD candidate at the University of Sydney. My research focuses on ensemble asteroseismology of red giants using Kepler, TESS, and Gaia data. Asteroseismic analysis enables us to infer fundamental stellar properties, including stellar masses, radii, and ages. The characterization of red giants is crucial for dating and mapping the Galaxy, and measuring the size of transiting exoplanets. I am also interested in pulsations and their effect on mass loss in long period variables, radial-velocity jitter due to intrinsic stellar variability, and super-Nyquist analysis.
